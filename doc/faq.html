Frequently Asked Questions

These are just Ant scripts, and I already have tasks for javadoc and PMD. Why should I use CodeFeedback?

The goal of CodeFeedback is to let you mix and match the sets of feedback you get from your code, and to keep you from having to write what is essentially boilerplate Ant scripts for many of these tools.

We already use a lot of these tools in Eclipse, and the feedback is much more immediate. Doesn't this make the feedback loop larger rather than smaller?
